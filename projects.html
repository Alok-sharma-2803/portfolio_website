Sales Funnel Conversion & Leakage Analysis

Sales Analytics · SQL · Power BI · Funnel Modeling
Funnel Analysis Conversion Metrics Data Quality
Context
  • Leadership lacked visibility into where deals were leaking across funnel stages
  • Existing reports emphasized volume over conversion efficiency
Approach
  • Built a stage-normalized funnel model in SQL
  • Standardized rules for re-entries, stage skips, and attribution
  • Calculated stage-level conversion rates and time-in-stage metrics
Insights & Impact
  • Identified early-stage leakage as the primary revenue constraint
  • Exposed overstatement of mid-funnel health due to re-entry inflation
  • Informed Sales Ops focus on qualification quality and early intervention
