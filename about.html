About Me

I design analytics systems that help leaders make better decisions — by combining metric governance, clear storytelling, and business-first thinking.

Professional Overview

I’m an analytics professional with 6+ years of experience supporting revenue, sales, and go-to-market teams in high-growth environments. My work sits at the intersection of executive reporting, KPI standardization, and decision enablement.

I focus on solving ambiguous business problems — turning fragmented data and inconsistent metrics into trusted systems leaders can rely on for planning, performance management, and operating reviews.

What I Typically Own

  • Defining and governing core business metrics in partnership with Finance and Ops
  • Designing executive dashboards that replace ad-hoc and spreadsheet-driven reporting
  • Building analytical models to support capacity planning, goal-setting, and prioritization
  • Translating leadership questions into structured analytical frameworks
  • Creating reusable reporting patterns that scale across teams

How I Work

  • Start with decision context, not visuals or tools
  • Optimize for clarity, consistency, and long-term usability
  • Partner closely with stakeholders to align on definitions and tradeoffs
  • Bias toward simple, explainable models over fragile complexity
  • Design analytics that enable action — not just observation
